Football History

Origins of Football

Ancient Beginnings

Games similar to football were played in China (cuju), Greece, and Rome over 2000 years ago.

Medieval Football

Rough and chaotic forms of football were played in England during the Middle Ages, often between rival villages.

Rise of Modern Football

The FA & Rules (1863)

The Football Association (FA) was founded in England in 1863, establishing standardized rules and separating football from rugby.

Global Expansion

Football spread quickly across Europe and South America, becoming a global sport by the early 20th century.

World Cup & Beyond

First FIFA World Cup (1930)

Held in Uruguay, the first World Cup was won by the hosts. It became the most prestigious football tournament globally.

Women's Football

Despite early bans, women’s football grew in popularity. The first FIFA Women’s World Cup was held in 1991.
